What's SmartLab?

SmartLab is a unified platform that simplifies laboratory operations in engineering colleges. Schedule practical sessions, track equipment with QR codes, automate attendance, and generate institutional reports — all in a secure, role-based system.

  • Role-based dashboards for Students, Faculty, Lab Assistants, Admins and Principals.
  • QR & Inventory tracking for equipment lifecycle and maintenance history.
  • Analytics and exportable reports for data-driven decision-making.
Learn more

Why SmartLab?

Built for institutions — from students to principal

  • Save faculty time by automating attendance & schedule approvals.
  • Improve utilization by eliminating booking conflicts and idle equipment.
  • Prevent equipment loss with QR-based checkouts and maintenance logs.
  • Support decisions with consolidated analytics for budgets, utilization, and performance.
